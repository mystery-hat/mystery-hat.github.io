Course Code: ANNUM101

Mentors : Professor Meghna Chauhan, Professor Chris Violette and Professor N.D. Bhatt

Course Code: TRIODE103

Mentors : Professor Dharmaa Maurya, Professor Chris Violette and Professor Arjun Jain

The Triode(I) Course is a course of 3-months duration for class 11th students. The course classes are held in the vacation months, viz. June, July and December. Course Code: APPARATE104
